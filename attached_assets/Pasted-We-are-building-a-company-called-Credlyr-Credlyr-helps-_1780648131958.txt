We are building a company called Credyr.

Credyr helps service businesses turn online attention into booked conversations.

The core insight is that buyer behavior has changed. People no longer browse 10–15 websites and carefully compare every option. They ask AI, search quickly, click one or two links, and make a fast trust decision. That means a company’s website is no longer just a digital brochure. It is now the trust layer between attention and revenue.

Credyr exists to help businesses win that moment.

We help companies improve how they present themselves online, capture leads, respond faster, qualify prospects, book appointments, and follow up automatically. The goal is simple: more trust, faster response, and more booked conversations without needing extra staff.

We do not position ourselves as a generic AI agency. We do not lead with “AI tools” or technical buzzwords. We sell business outcomes: better websites, better lead handling, faster follow-up, and more appointments.

The first major offer under Credyr is a lead response and booking system. It helps businesses respond instantly when someone submits a form, calls, messages, or shows interest. The system can qualify the lead, answer basic questions, book an appointment, update the CRM, and follow up if the person does not respond.

The bigger vision is that Credyr becomes a modern conversion partner for service businesses. We help them upgrade from static websites and slow manual follow-up to a full conversion system:

Website → Lead Capture → Instant Response → Qualification → Booking → CRM → Follow-up → Revenue

Credyr is for service businesses where trust and speed matter. Examples include insurance brokers, dentists, orthodontists, beauty salons, med spas, home service companies, real estate businesses, and local professional service firms.

The brand should feel premium, clear, modern, and trustworthy. It should not feel like a cheap automation agency or a technical AI startup. The message should be easy to understand in a few seconds:

Credyr helps service businesses convert more online visitors into booked appointments through better websites, faster lead response, and automated follow-up.
